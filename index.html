DISCOVER HIDDEN GEMS

Escape the crowds and explore three secluded, untouched locations in the stunning High Tatras. These spots remain free from over-tourism, offering pure tranquility and natural beauty. Visit our subpages for descriptions and tips to find these hidden treasures.

Zelené Pleso (Green Lake)

Zelené Pleso is a stunning alpine lake located in the High Tatras of Slovakia, known for its emerald-green waters surrounded by dramatic peaks.

How to get there: Start from the village of Tatranská Lomnica or Kežmarské Žľaby. Follow the well-marked yellow trail leading through the beautiful valley of Dolina Zeleného plesa. The hike takes approximately 3-4 hours one way, depending on your starting point, and offers breathtaking views along the way.

Javorová Dolina (Maple Valley)

Javorová Dolina is a picturesque valley in the High Tatras, renowned for its serene beauty, lush forests, and panoramic views.

How to get there: The valley is accessible from the village of Tatranská Javorina. Follow the blue-marked hiking trail that gently ascends through the valley. The hike takes approximately 2-3 hours one way, offering stunning vistas of alpine meadows, crystal-clear streams, and dramatic mountain scenery.

Svišťový Štít (Marmot Peak)

Svišťový Štít is a majestic peak in the High Tatras, offering breathtaking views of rugged mountain terrain and surrounding valleys.

How to get there: The route to Svišťový Štít begins in Tatranská Lomnica or Kežmarské Žľaby, with a hike through Javorová Dolina. Follow the blue trail into the valley, then continue on unmarked paths to the peak, where you can see wonderful views.
